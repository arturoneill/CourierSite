Personal Courier and Errand Services

  • - Point-to-point on-demand delivery (mail and package pickup and dropoff, rent checks, office and art supplies, books, convenience and grocery store runs, alcohol, paperwork, flowers, graphics, samples, designs, gift delivery, clothing, interoffice work, computers and electronics, quick shopping errands, bank deposits, restaurant pickup and delivery, and more)

  • rates

    • $6/8 Inner/Outer Zone rush fee (p/u and d/o within ~1hr of p/u ready time)
    • $4/6 Inner/Outer Zone standard fee (p/u and d/o within ~2hr of p/u ready time)
    • $3/5 Inner/Outer Zone all-day fee (p/u and d/o within ~4hr of p/u ready time, unavailable after 4pm weekdays and 1pm Saturdays)
    • +$4 Shopping for >10 items
    • +$4 Deliveries >48 linear inches or >20lbs total
    • +$4 Extra Stop
    • +$4 Shopping/waiting time fee (after first 15min grace)
    • +10% Catering fee for restaurant purchases >$75 total
  • - See map for layout of zones and to check location of addresses within zones
  • - Please label or have your contact label envelopes and packages with drop-off contact name, business name, address, and phone number and other applicable contact information to ensure correct completion and receipt of your delivery.
  • - Prepayment of purchases recommended, although we can front payment of purchases up to $100 if necessary
  • - Multiple options for payment of fees and fronted purchases (cash, credit or debit via Square, PayPal, or Venmo) at pick-up, drop-off, or over the phone
  • - Credit and debit card charges subject to Square, PayPal, or Venmo processing fees
  • - Electronic or paper signature, call, text, or email on delivery available upon request
  • - Pickups from popular businesses subject to potential delays at peak hours
  • - Most restaurant, grocery, flower, and any other especially fragile deliveries are generally rush ~1hr deliveries and charged accordingly
  • - Alcohol delivery subject to electronic ID verification and limited to “12 bottles” by Florida law
  • - Ask about delivery outside of Inner/Outer Zones
