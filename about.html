ABOUT

WHAT WE DO

We are a sustainable, affordable, and efficient employee-owned bike-based courier company servicing downtown Gainesville, UF campus, and neighboring areas.

We have 20 combined years of experience in delivering food and flowers, parcel courier service, bike maintenance, and running organizations. We intend to pour all of this into providing you with a professional courier service, along with a personal level of business support.

We offer a range of services meeting your personal and business demands including rush delivery, errand shopping, catering solutions, gift delivery, and virtually anything else you can think of.

Contact us for a detailed list of rates and see if we can work for you. We are an agile, lightweight company prepared to bring hard-hitting, local competition against the multinational corporations trying to move in.
