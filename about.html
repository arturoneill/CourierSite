About

With 7 years of previous experience delivering food, flowers, paperwork, and everything in between, Palmetto Courier was started by Zach Sorensen in 2017. We are a sustainable, affordable, and efficient employee-owned bike-based courier company servicing downtown Gainesville, UF campus, and neighboring areas.
